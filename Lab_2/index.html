An Ayurveda-approved ice cream that is high in protein, sweetened only with fruit, and contains brain-boosting herbs. This ice cream can help ease PMS symptoms and balance hormones.

Ingredients

List of Ingredients

 1.  2 cups of frozen cauliflower.
 2. One cup of frozen blueberries.
 3. One scoop of any good bone broth protein.
 4. One scoop Anima Mundi Qi Energy Powder
 5. One scoop Anima Mundi Shroom Mocha Milk
 6. Add ½ tsp ginger, cinnamon, and cardamom to spice it up and further reduce the Kapha
 7. 2 cups water, plus more if needed.
 8. Blend in a blender until smooth.
